## Summery
Hi! I’m Yufei Huang, a Ph.D. student in Computer Science at Zhejiang University ([Resume](./Yufei_Huang_CV.pdf)). \textbf{My research focuses on Unified Multi-modal World Model (UMM) and Generative Models.} As shown in Figure 1, my long-term vision is to build Creative Intelligence capable of scalable multi-modal reasoning and interaction, \textbf{enabling persistent simulation of immersive worlds with reality-level spatiotemporal structure}. 

To realize this vision, \textbf{my road-map decomposes UMM into three aspects:} formulation (world model), generalizable framework (to unify understanding, generation and multi-modality), and objectives (end-to-end generation as universal rewards). \textbf{My current focus is long video generation}, studying continuity, long-horizon control, and consistency under video length scaling. My research aims to address frontier problems through fundamental insights.
